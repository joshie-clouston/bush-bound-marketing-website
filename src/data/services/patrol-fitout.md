## What's included

The Patrol wagon has one of the largest cargo areas in the 4WD class. The Y61 GU gives you roughly 1,100mm of cargo depth (seats up) with about 1,200mm of width, and the Y62 is even wider. That kind of space means you can run a serious drawer system, a full fridge setup, and still have room for recovery gear and water.

Our Patrol builds include a twin-drawer system with integrated fridge slide, a sleeping platform option for touring setups, and a 12V power system. For the Y61 wagon, we can build a full rear fit-out that turns the cargo area into a proper self-contained camp kitchen and storage system. The Y62 gets a more refined build that matches the vehicle's premium interior.

All cabinetry is CNC-cut from premium plywood and finished with our hand-lacquered waterproof coating. The builds are designed to handle the weight of loaded drawers on rough tracks without flexing, cracking, or rattling.

## Why the Patrol

The Patrol is a beast of a touring vehicle. The Y61 GU with the 4.2L turbo diesel is arguably the most capable off-road wagon ever made, and the Y62 brings V8 power with modern comfort. Both have massive cargo capacity and a loyal following in the Australian touring community.

Our builds suit Patrol owners who want organised storage and a proper camp setup without bolting a rooftop tent to the roof or towing a trailer. The sheer volume inside a Patrol wagon means you can carry everything you need for weeks, as long as it's organised properly. That's where we come in.
