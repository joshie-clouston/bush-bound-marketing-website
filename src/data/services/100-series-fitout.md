## What's included

The 100 Series has a deep cargo well behind the third row, roughly 1,000mm deep, 1,200mm wide, and 750mm tall to the cargo cover. With the third row folded, you get close to 1,900mm of usable length. The deep floor well is both a blessing and a challenge. It's great for volume, but without a proper drawer system you end up stacking gear on top of gear.

Our 100 Series builds typically start with a dual-drawer system that sits at the top of the cargo well, creating a flat platform at the window line. Below the drawers, you've still got the deep well for bulky items like recovery gear, water containers, and swags. We add a fridge slide on one side, sized for your specific fridge model.

For touring setups, we build a sleeping platform that extends from the drawer system forward over the folded third-row seats. Combined with a 12V power system and our hand-lacquered finish, it's a complete weekend touring package.

## Why the 100 Series

The 100 Series LandCruiser is one of the most loved touring vehicles in Australia. They're tough, comfortable on long drives, and have a parts ecosystem that'll keep them on the road for decades. The 1HD-FTE diesel and the 2UZ-FE V8 are both proven powertrains with huge aftermarket support.

Our builds are designed to complement a vehicle that many owners plan to keep for life. The CNC-cut plywood cabinetry looks premium, fits precisely, and removes cleanly if you ever need to go back to stock. For a vehicle that's often worth more built up than stock, a quality fit-out is a genuine investment.
