## What's included

The 200 Series has a cargo area roughly 1,060mm deep (third row up), 1,250mm wide, and 800mm to the cargo cover. With the third row folded into the floor, you get about 1,940mm of usable length. The flat-fold third row is a big advantage over the 100 Series as it gives you a much cleaner floor to build on.

Our 200 Series builds include a twin-drawer system with fridge slide, designed to sit flush with the window line so you get a clean, flat load surface on top. The drawers run on heavy-duty ball-bearing slides rated for rough tracks, and the fridge slide locks in both open and closed positions.

For full touring setups, we add a sleeping platform, 12V power with lithium battery and management system, and a slide-out kitchen module. The 200's KDSS suspension and general road manners make it an exceptional long-distance tourer, and our builds are designed to match that level of refinement. No rough edges, no exposed screws, no rattle.

## Why the 200 Series

The 200 Series is the gold standard for Australian touring wagons. The 4.5L twin-turbo V8 diesel has more than enough power for loaded touring, the suspension handles everything from highways to corrugated outback roads, and the cabin is genuinely comfortable for long days behind the wheel.

These vehicles hold their value exceptionally well, and a quality fit-out only adds to that. Our CNC-cut plywood builds bring a level of craftsmanship that matches the vehicle's premium positioning. Whether you're running a GXL or a Sahara, the fit-out looks and feels like it was designed by Toyota themselves.
