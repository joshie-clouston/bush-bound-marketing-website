## What we build

The 78 Series Troop Carrier is the ultimate Australian expedition vehicle. That high-roof cargo area gives you something no wagon or ute canopy can match, and we build full expedition interiors using CNC-cut premium plywood, designed in 3D and finished to handle years of corrugations, creek crossings, and red dust.

Every Troopy build starts with a conversation about how you actually use your vehicle. Weekend warrior? Full-time tourer? Work truck that doubles as a camper? That changes everything, from sleeping layout to power requirements to where we put the water tank.

Our builds are designed to be removed without permanent modification to the vehicle. That means no cutting into the body, no drilling where it's not needed, and full resale value when it's time to move on.
