## What's included

HiLux canopy builds are all about working with the tray dimensions. A dual-cab N80 HiLux gives you roughly 1,525mm of tray length, 1,540mm of width, and then whatever height your canopy adds on top. That's a compact footprint, so the design has to be sharp.

Our standard HiLux setup includes a full-width drawer system with a fridge slide, a sleeping platform that sits above the drawers and extends past the tailgate with a fold-out section, and a 12V power system tucked into the build. We can add a slide-out kitchen module on either side depending on your preference and canopy door configuration.

We design around your specific canopy brand and model. FlexiGlass, ARB, EGR, Razorback, or any other brand. The internal dimensions vary between manufacturers, so we measure your actual vehicle and canopy before cutting anything.

## Why the HiLux

The HiLux is the best-selling vehicle in Australia for a reason. It's tough, parts are everywhere, and with a canopy it becomes a genuinely capable touring platform. The tray separation means your living setup stays clean and separate from the cabin, and you can run a completely self-contained camping system in the back.

Our plywood builds bring a level of finish to a HiLux canopy that metal or plastic drawer systems just don't have. Everything is precision-cut to your tray and canopy dimensions, hand-lacquered for waterproofing, and designed to handle the vibration and movement that comes with ute touring.
