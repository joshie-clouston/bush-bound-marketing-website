## What's included

The Multivan shares its body with the Transporter but comes with the factory floor rail system, sliding rear seats, and often a fold-flat rear bench. Our builds are designed to work with these existing features rather than ripping them out.

A typical Multivan build includes a rear kitchen module that sits behind the sliding seat, a compact storage system that utilises the space under the fold-flat bed, and a 12V power setup. We design the cabinetry to lock into the factory rail system where possible, so the whole setup can be repositioned or removed when you need the van for other duties.

Because many Multivan owners use the van as a family vehicle during the week, our builds prioritise quick conversion. You should be able to go from people-mover to camper in under ten minutes without tools.

## Why the Multivan

The Multivan is the pick if you need one vehicle to do everything. School runs, work commute, weekend camping, road trips. The factory seating and rail system give you flexibility that a stripped-out Transporter doesn't have.

Our builds lean into that versatility. We design modular setups that slot in around the existing seats, so you never have to choose between passengers and camping gear. The CNC-cut plywood components are precision-fit to the vehicle, and the hand-lacquered finish means everything looks sharp whether you're parked at school pickup or at a campsite.
