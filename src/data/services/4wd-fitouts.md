## What we build

Not every build needs to be a full live-in setup. For 4WD wagons and utes, it's often about smart storage, a solid drawer system, and a setup that keeps your gear organised and accessible when you're off the beaten track.

We build everything from simple drawer systems and sleeping platforms through to full rear cargo fit-outs with slide-out kitchens, water tanks, and 12V power. All CNC-cut from premium plywood, designed in 3D, and finished with our hand-lacquered waterproof coating.

Whether you're running a Prado for weekend camping, a HiLux with a canopy for longer trips, or a Patrol as a tow rig that doubles as a tourer, we'll build something that fits the vehicle and fits your life.

## Vehicles we work with

- **76 Series Wagon** - The short-wheelbase 70 Series. Compact but capable, perfect for couples who want 70 Series reliability without the length.
- **Toyota Prado 150** - One of the most popular touring platforms in Australia. We build compact, efficient rear cargo setups that maximise the available space.
- **Toyota HiLux** - Canopy builds with drawer systems, fridge slides, and sleeping platforms. Works well as a weekender or a proper touring rig.
- **Nissan Patrol Y61/Y62** - Massive cargo area, especially the Y61 wagon. Great platform for a full rear fit-out.
- **LandCruiser 100 Series** - A cult classic. We build drawer systems and cargo setups that make the most of that deep cargo well.
- **LandCruiser 200 Series** - Premium touring wagon. Our builds complement the vehicle's existing quality.
