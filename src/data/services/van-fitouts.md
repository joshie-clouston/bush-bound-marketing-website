## What we build

Van conversions are about making every centimetre count. Whether you're building a weekender, a full-time home on wheels, or a work van that converts for trips away, we design and build interiors that are practical, good-looking, and built to last.

All our van builds use CNC-cut premium plywood, designed in 3D so you can see exactly what you're getting before we cut a single sheet. We hand-lacquer every surface for a waterproof finish that handles spills, humidity, and the general chaos of van life.

We're not about cookie-cutter conversions. Every build is designed around how you'll actually use the van. Daily driver that needs to convert quickly? Full-time living setup with proper storage? Surf wagon with a shower? Tell us what matters and we'll design around it.

## Vehicles we work with

- **VW Transporter T5/T6** - The gold standard for compact van conversions. Great dimensions for a couple's setup with kitchen, bed, and storage.
- **VW Multivan** - Similar platform to the Transporter but with the factory rail system. We work with and around the existing seating to keep flexibility.
- **Toyota HiAce** - More cargo volume than the VW, especially in SLWB form. Popular choice for taller people or anyone who wants a bit more room to move.
